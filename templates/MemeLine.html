What's a Meme?

A meme is an image, video, or piece of text that is copied and often modified and virally spread.

This site will focus on "Internet Memes" and how they came about. The timeline below displays an iconic meme from the year at which it shows. The dates are the time when the images were "invented". This date is when the world first got a glimpse of them. There is no out date when it comes to memes and they are still widely used today for come-back lines or fun overall.
